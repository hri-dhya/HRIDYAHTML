Kochi

Kochi, formerly called as Cochin, is a major port city on the west coast of India by the Arabian Sea and is part of the district of Ernakulam in the state of Kerala.

Marindrive

Marine Drive Kochi is a great place to relax and enjoy the evening with friends and family. It offers beautiful sunset views and is a bustling hub with ships.

Queens way

Better than marine drive for an evening walk. Not that crowded well lit walkway with music playing. Good breeze. Nice ambience. Easily accessible.
